STUDIO

Photography equipment – space required 15’ x 15’ (225 square feet
Lights

One light and a reflector are more than enough to start. But three lights will give you the best flexibility.

Flash Trigger

This tool is necessary to trigger your flash units remotely. You’ll need a specific model that’s compatible with your strobes and your camera.

Lighting modifiers

Reflector and umbrella are required. Neewer and Godox make cheap umbrellas, softboxes, and octaboxes. They are pretty good and fold into small, easy-to-storage bags.

Light stands

The stands that extend to 2.4 metres and higher. Especially since the lights need to be above the model. This can light the model from below or hide your rim/background light behind the model.

Background

A collapsible background is a good way to start. Impact and other brands are not too expensive. Especially when compared to Lastoline collapsible backdrops.
